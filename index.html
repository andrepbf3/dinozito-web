Common Questions

Where do you ship to?

We ship worldwide through Amazon. Your book will be printed and shipped from the nearest Amazon facility for faster delivery to your door.

What is the paper quality?

DinoZito has been optimized for colored pencils and fine liners. Our high-quality paper is designed to make those HD details pop!

If you prefer using heavier markers or alcohol-based pens, we recommend placing a blank sheet of paper or cardstock behind the page you’re coloring. This ensures the ink won’t bleed through to the next prehistoric adventure.

What age group is this for?

DinoZito is specially designed for children aged 3 to 9 years old. However, art has no age limit! Whether you are a parent coloring with your child or an adult looking for a relaxing creative escape, our professional designs offer a peaceful experience for everyone.
