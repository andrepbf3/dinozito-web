Common Questions

Where do you ship to?

We ship worldwide through Amazon. Whether you are in Brazil, Portugal, USA, or Spain, your book will be printed and shipped from the nearest Amazon facility.

What is the paper quality?

Our books feature high-quality outlines on single-sided pages. This prevents bleed-through and allows kids to use crayons, colored pencils, or even light markers without ruining the next drawing.

What age group is this for?

The DinoZito Professions collection is perfect for children aged 4 to 10. The drawings are clear enough for younger kids, but detailed enough to keep older children engaged.
